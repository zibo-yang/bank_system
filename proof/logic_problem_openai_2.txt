\[
M_n = H \oplus 0_{n-2}.

\[
\rho_q(M_n)=2^{1/2-1/q}

for all relevant \(q\ge 2\), and Subproblem 4 then gives
\[
\rho_p(M_n)<\rho_{p+1}(M_n).

No missing cases are apparent: since \(p>2\), both \(p\) and \(p+1\) lie in the range \(q\ge2\), and since \(n>6\), the block embedding into dimension \(n\) is covered by Subproblem 3.
